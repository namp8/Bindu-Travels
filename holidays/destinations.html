Holiday Destinations

At Bindu Travels & Tours Ltd , we endeavour to tailor-make unique holidays with personalised Itineraries.

Whether you’re seeking adventure or culture, celebrating a milestone event or honeymoon, we’ll ensure your holiday is special, memorable and exactly what you dreamed of.

  • DUBAI

    A very dynamic, international business centre and a relaxing tourist destination, offers many great entertainment opportunities for families from theme parks and water slides to ice skating and sandy beaches.

    Combine your stay with a 7 night Arabian Gulf Cruise to neighbouring states of Oman and Bahrain or stay at the Lapita Hotel and experience Middle East’s largest leisure and theme park.

    With three theme parks and one waterpark, Dubai Parks and Resorts, offers visitors a thrilling experience like no other. Visit IMG Worlds of Adventure, the world’s largest temperature-controlled indoor themed entertainment destination in the world, with a capacity to welcome more than 20,000 guests a day. With four epic adventure zones, two of which have been created with global adventure brands Cartoon Network and MARVEL, while IMG Boulevard and Lost Valley-Dinosaur Adventure are original concepts from the IMG Group.

  • Burj Al Arab
  • Burj Khalifa
  • Atlantis Palm
  • Ski Dubai
  • Dubai Parks
  • Desert Safari
  • Gold Souk
  • Dubai Mall
  • IMG World of Adventure

  • INDIA

    The birthplace of Yoga and Ayurveda, India is a country steeped in history and colour.

    Explore the south indian paradise of Kerala in all its glory with Hill stations, tea plantations, Ayurveda and cruise the backwaters on a houseboat, watching the jungle and rice-paddies float by. Also visit the wildlife sanctuary of Periyar.

    Take a journey on the luxurious train, The Palace on Wheels, to explore the splendours of Rajastan, Agra and Delhi.

    Explore India’s capital, Delhi and visit the glorious India Gate, The grand Red Fort, The mysterious ruins of Qutub Minar, Humayun’s Tomb, Raj Ghat – the resting place of Mahatma Gandhi and bustling Chandni Chowk.

    Unwind on the beaches of Goa or enjoy the vibrancy of Mumbai and the bazaars and shopping …

  • Kerala
  • Udaipur
  • Delhi
  • Agra
  • Jaipur
  • Goa

  • THAILAND

    Is known for tropical beaches, opulent royal palaces, ancient ruins and ornate temples displaying figures of Buddha.

    The Capital city Bangkok, is a non-stop whirlwind of entertainment, wonderful hospitality, its pulsating nightlife and vibrant markets reflect the Thais determination to enjoy life to the fullest.

    Steeped in tradition and history, Bangkok possesses the iconic temples of Wat Pho, with the opulent reclining Buddha and the Emerald Buddha Temple. Nearby beach resorts include bustling Pattaya, Phuket and fashionable Hua Hin or head to Koh Tao Or Ko Samui for authentic island getaways.

    Also explore the temples of Chiang Mai or go Elephant Trekking.

  • Bangkok
  • Koh Samui
  • Chiang mai

  • HONG KONG

    Renowed for its glittering skyline and magnificent harbour, Hongkong is one of the most stimulating cities. With towering skyscrapers, a blend of ethnic cultures and native cuisines, Hong Kong attracts a large number of tourists every year.

    Take an exciting tram ride to Victoria Peak, go on a cable car up to Ngong Ping Village and take in the spectacular view of the surroundings. Also see the The Big Buddha in Lantau Island, explore the magic of Hongkong Disneyland and discover the attractions, thrill rides and marine life at Ocean Park.

  • Disneyland
  • Ocean park
  • Victoria Peak

  • SINGAPORE

    One of the most multicultural destinations in Southeast Asia, is steeped in history and has fun-packed family appeal too, from the island attractions of Sentosa to one of the only night-time safaris in the world.

    Explore other touristic sites such as Gardens by the Bay, The Singapore Flyer, Singapore Zoo , Jurong Bird Park and no trip to Singapore is complete without a visit to Sentosa Island and Universal Studios as well as shopping on the famous Orchard Street.

  • Sentosa Island
  • Universal Studios
  • Singapore flyer
  • Jurong bird park

  • MALAYSIA

    From the buzzing capital of Kuala Lumpur to the tropics of Borneo, Malaysia has a lot to offer, encompassing rugged mountains, tropical rainforest, almost 900 islands and mix of Malay, Chinese, Indian and European cultural influences.

    The capital, Kuala Lumpur, is home to colonial buildings, busy shopping districts such as Bukit Bintang and skyscrapers such as the iconic Petronas Twin Towers.

    Explore the rainforest & beaches in Kota Kinabalu, the sandy beaches of Langkawi , Redang & Tioman Islands, take the kids for a day trip to Sunway Lagoon or on thrilling rides at LEGOLAND Malaysia Resort and Water Park, located in Johor Bahru.

    Enjoy an ideal family weekend in the Genting Highlands with breathtaking views and a theme park with rides and attractions for adults and children.

  • Petronas twin towers
  • Langkawi
  • Penang
  • Genting Highlands

  • MACAU

    An astounding mixture of colonial charm, Chinese zest and glitzy casinos is just a short one-hour ferry ride from Hong Kong.

    It offers something different, whether it is enticing Portuguese and Macanese cooking, striking colonial architecture, a UNESCO World Heritage city center, a hand at the baccarat tables or stint at the slot machines... it is well worth the trek from Hong Kong.

    Go on a weekend trip and explore outstanding Luxurious Hotels & Resorts in Macau such as the Altira, Banyan Tree , Ritz Carlton, MGM, Mandarin Oriental ,The Venetian.. to name a few.

    And be sure to catch the largest water extravaganza at City of Dreams in Macau, The House Of Dancing Water, by Franco Dragone.

  • Venetian hotel

  • CHINA

    Has a host of must-see attractions, ranging from spectacular natural wonders to some of the world’s most impressive man-made structures. There’s also a wealth of history, culture and cuisine for all to enjoy.

    Top of the bucket list for most first time visitors, is the iconic Great Wall of China.

    Beijing, China’s massive capital, is known as much for its modern architecture as its ancient sites such as the grand Forbidden City complex, the Imperial Palace during the Ming and Qing dynasties.

    Shanghai - China's biggest city, oozes an atmosphere of vitality which can rival New York and Paris in terms of modernity.

    Hangzhou's "heavenly" beauty attracts hundreds of thousands of travellers. There are also convenient high-speed trains from/to Shanghai.

    Shenzhen, also known as a shopping destination, houses ‘The Window of the World’ ,a theme park featuring small replications of the Seven Wonders of the World and famous landmarks.

  • Great wall of china
  • Schenzen
  • Shanghai

  • EGYPT

    A country linking northeast Africa with the Middle East, dates to the time of the pharaohs. Millennia-old monuments sit along the Nile River Valley, including Giza's colossal Pyramids and Great Sphinx as well as Luxor's Karnak Temple and Valley of the Kings tombs. The capital, Cairo, is home to landmarks like Muhammad Ali Mosque, the Egyptian Museum and the busy and colourful open-air bazaar ‘ Khan El-Khalili ‘ filled with unique and exotic items.

    A Cruise on the mighty river Nile is one of the best ways to discover Egypt.

  • Nile Cruise
  • Pyramids
  • Sphinx of Giza
  • Sharm El Sheikh
  • Hurghada
  • Alexandria
  • Aswan
  • Luxor

  • TANZANIA

    A Safari Mecca , populated by the “big five” game and Kilimanjaro National Park , is also home to Africa’s highest mountain.

    A safari here is truly memorable,with remote fly-in lodges, charming camps in beautiful settings and of course the Big Five wildlife to spot.

    Witnessing ‘the great migration’of wildebeest between the Masai Mara in Kenya and the Serengeti in Tanzania is a once in a lifetime experience

    Then head to the clear, turquoise –blue waters of Zanzibar. Also explore the centre of Stone Town, where you can enjoy the buzz of a seaside marina and spend hours perusing the handmade art and craft stalls.

  • Arusha
  • Kilimanjaro National Park
  • Zanzibar
  • Serengeti National Park
  • Ngorongoro Crater
  • Wildebeest Migration

  • KENYA

    A country in East Africa, encompasses savannah, lakelands, the dramatic Great Rift Valley and mountain highlands.

    Kenya offers a fantastic safari destination to enjoy a diversity of incredible and iconic African wildlife . From Nairobi, the capital, visit the Maasai Mara Reserve, known for its annual wildebeest migrations, and Amboseli National Park, offering views of Tanzania's 5,895m Mt. Kilimanjaro.

  • Maasai Mara
  • Lake Nakuru
  • Diani Beach
  • Mombasa
  • Malindi

  • SOUTH AFRICA

    A land of great diversity and incredible beauty, “ a world in one country” South Africa is one of the most sought after travel destinations in the world. Its beauty is breathed through the smiles of friendly faces, the mosaic of cultures and cuisines, and the complex history that has shaped it.

    SA has almost 3000km of beautiful coastline along with golfing, hiking, climbing and endless opportunities for shopping and fine /casual dining for everyone.

    Capetown, is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful cities in the world, here you can ride the cable car and marvel at the view from the top of Table Mountain. The sensational Victoria &Alfred Waterfront is very lively with fun activities, shopping and countless restaurants.

    No trip is complete without a wine tasting tour to Stellenbosch & Franschhoek.

    Kruger National Park is one of the world’s most famous safari parks and the Garden Route is a playground of beautiful beaches, coastal forest and world-class golfing.

  • Table Mountain
  • Cape of Good Hope
  • Boulders Beach
  • Robben Island
  • Kruger National Park
  • V&A waterfront
  • Wine Tasting

  • TURKEY

    Is a dazzling destination combining East with West with its vibrant culture, famous food and vast history.

    Visit Istanbul’s Old Quarter, with its lively grand bazaar, atmospheric restaurants and architectural marvels such as the Blue Mosque, Hagia Sophia and Topkapi Palace.

    Cappadocia is equally intriguing with its ancient underground cities.

    If you’re looking for a more relaxed break, head to Antalya, the ‘Turkish Riviera’ for pristine beaches, lively nightlife and national parks.

  • Antalaya
  • Blue Mosque
  • Hagia Sofia
  • Bosporous
  • Grand Bazaar
  • Ankara
  • Bodrum
  • Ephesus
  • Galata Tower

  • THE MALDIVES, MAURITIUS AND SEYCHELLES

    The Maldives, Mauritius and Seychelles are long time favourites. The private islands of the Maldives offer overwater villas, private dining, sunset cruises, spa treatments as well as snorkelling and diving.

    Mauritius, a sparkling crystal in the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, will fascinate you. The contrast of colours, cultures and tastes makes the island so charming that the scene is set for an unforgettable holiday.

    The relaxing Seychelles with its picture perfect beaches offers island hopping to explore the protected marine and nature reserves as well as deep sea fishing.

  • The Maldives
  • Seychelles
  • Ile Aux Cerfs Island
  • Seven Colored Earths
  • Grand Baie
  • Ile Aux 2 Cocos
  • Port Louis
  • Casela

  • SRI LANKA

    Discover the most beautiful sights with history, nature and beauty , this fascinating small island country has so much to offer!

    Bursting with beaches and temples, blessed with wonderful wildlife, packed with UNESCO-listed sites and filled with fascinating and colourful culture, Sri Lanka is truly spectacular.

    Colombo, the largest city and capital, is a mix of modern business establishments and colonial buildings and temples. Highlights include Independence Square, Galle Face and Petah Bazaar. Indulge in shopping for traditional handicrafts.

    Lovely Kandy, rich in cultural diversity, and the site of the Temple Of the Tooth. Climb the 5th century Sigiriya Rock Fortress , or visit the elephant orphange in Pinnawala, home to about 100 elephants. Explore a tea plantation in Nuwara Eliya.

  • Sigiriya Rock
  • Anuradhapura
  • Polonnaruwa Temples
  • Budha statues
  • Mirissa
  • Kalpitiya
  • Galle Dutch Forts
  • Arugambay
  • N'Eliya
  • Diyaluma Falls
  • Yala Safari
  • Adams peak

  • BALI

    An Indonesian island, is known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches and coral reefs. The island is home to religious sites such as cliffside Uluwatu Temple. To the south, the beachside city of Kuta has lively bars, while Seminyak, Sanur and Nusa Dua are popular resort towns. The island is also known for its yoga, meditation retreats and rejuvenating spas.

  • Tanah Lot
  • Tirta Empul
  • Uluwatu temple
  • Rock Bar
  • A villa in Seminyak
