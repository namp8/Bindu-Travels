10 Brilliant reasons why your next break should be a cruising holiday!

  • The price is almost fully inclusive. The cost of your cruise includes your accommodation, transportation, all meals and entertainment. In some cases it will include gratuities too. This takes away the stress of paying for surprise expenses like extra meals, taxis, buses, room service, incidentals etc.
  • The floating resorts offer you a different destination each day. Every morning you wake up in a new country or island waiting to be explored. The ship offers optional organised excursions or you may go out on your own.
  • You enjoy some of the best dining & entertainment in the world. Every day on board ship is different. Different theme days, menus & shows offer you a unique experience every night. You never have to do the same thing twice.
  • You only unpack & pack once! You can visit different destinations without living out of a suitcase, or spending most of your holiday running from one airport to the next, catching buses and taxis.
  • On board the ship the whole family can enjoy a proper holiday. Married or single, with or without children, the modern floating resorts offer facilities & entertainment for different needs.
  • Modern cruise ships offer a very safe environment. There are no incidents of crime on board.
  • You can visit the most exotic places without sacrificing any of the conveniences you are accustomed to. You can visit the Far East, Russia, Chili, Argentina, the Amazon & the Rain Forests but every evening you will come home to your modern & comfortable resort and exchange experiences with you fellow guests over a sundowner followed by a lavish 5 course dinner & a broadway show!
  • Cruising is not only for the rich. It is cheaper to enjoy a cruise holiday than similar land based one. With prices starting from as little as US$90.00 per person per day, cruising offers unbeatable value for money!
  • Cruising is not only for the old. No matter what lifestyle you lead our floating resorts offer you so many different activities and entertainment that it is certain you will be offered to do what you enjoy doing. No matter how active or inactive you want your holiday to be, our resorts will offer you exactly that! Tennis, mini golf, video arcade, basketball, skid shooting, libraries, video libraries, card games, aerobics, fully equipped gyms, health spas, message, facials and much more are on offer daily.
  • Cruising is guaranteed to be the most relaxed and enjoyable holiday of your life.
